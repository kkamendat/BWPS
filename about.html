Our Story

Blue Water Pool Service started in 1990 as an above ground swimming pool installation company. Our mission is to provide the highest-quality, best-supported pool services in Port Huron. We began as a pool installation company, but as our company grew and relationships were built, we expanded to offer all types of pool, spa, and hot tub services.

Our team stays current with all new technologies and is always ready to provide you with the latest in swimming pool products and expertise.
