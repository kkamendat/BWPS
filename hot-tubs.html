Hot Tubs & Swim Spas

Whether you already have a hot tub or swim spa — or are looking to install a new one — we’ve got you covered! Blue Water Pool Service offers expert installation, maintenance, and repair services to keep your spa in peak condition all year long.

  • Installation & Delivery: We handle every step, from site prep to setup, ensuring your hot tub or swim spa is installed safely and correctly.
  • Full Hot Tub Service: Keep your spa in top condition with thorough inspections, water treatment, and equipment maintenance.
  • Seasonal Openings & Closings: Protect your spa year-round with professional winterization and spring setup services.
  • Repairs & Troubleshooting: From pump issues to leaks and electrical problems, our expert technicians quickly diagnose and fix your spa.
  • Water Testing & Cleaning: Maintain perfect water chemistry and cleanliness with routine service and chemical balancing.
  • Custom Spa Covers: Preserve heat and keep debris out with our durable, made-to-fit hot tub and swim spa covers.

Serving Port Huron and surrounding areas with friendly, professional spa care.
