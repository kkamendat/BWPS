Hot Tubs & Swim Spas

Whether you already have a hot tub or swim spa — or are looking to install a new one — we’ve got you covered! Blue Water Pool Service offers expert installation, maintenance, and repair services to keep your spa in peak condition all year long.

  • Installation & Delivery: Safe, professional setup from start to finish.
  • Full Hot Tub Service: Inspections, water treatment, and equipment maintenance.
  • Seasonal Openings & Closings: Proper winterization and spring startup.
  • Repairs & Troubleshooting: Pumps, leaks, heaters, and electrical issues.
  • Water Testing & Cleaning: Balanced, crystal-clear water.
  • Custom Spa Covers: Durable, heat-retaining covers made to fit.
