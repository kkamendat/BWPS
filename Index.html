Welcome to Blue Water Pool Service

Frustrated by a recent swimming pool product failure? Need help with a pool refurbishing? Just purchased a new home — and want the perfect new pool installed? No matter your pool service needs, Blue Water Pool Service is ready to help.

For nearly 30 years, we’ve been helping homeowners in Port Huron keep their pools in tip-top shape. If you want to be able to relax and enjoy your pool — without having to worry about fixing or installing it — then Blue Water Pool Service is perfect for you.

Here’s how it works: When you call us, we’ll always have a staff member waiting to hear from you. We’re transparent about our availability and services, so you’ll know exactly when we can help and what we can do for you. Before our visit we will ask the questions that will help us give you good understanding of what services we will be performing and also help us to give you an accurate estimate of what those services will cost. And when it’s time to service your pool, we promise to show up on time and treat your home with the utmost professionalism.
