Specialty Equipment & Materials

Investing in the right specialty equipment can drastically enhance the functionality, safety, and enjoyment of your pool or spa. Our team at Blue Water Pool Service is here to guide you in selecting the best options to meet your specific needs. From energy-efficient pumps to custom safety covers, we offer a wide range of high-quality equipment to make pool ownership easier and more enjoyable.

We’ve got you covered for all your additional pool equipment needs:

  • Automation Systems: Simplify pool and spa maintenance with systems that can automatically control temperature, lighting, and filtration.
  • Specialty Timers: Save energy and ensure consistent maintenance schedules with customizable timers for pumps and heaters.
  • Custom Safety Covers: Protect your pool from debris and prevent accidents with our durable, custom-fit covers.
  • Custom Hot Tub Covers: Keep your hot tub water clean and warm while protecting it from weather elements with our custom covers.
  • Variable Speed Pumps: Reduce energy consumption and operating costs with energy-efficient variable speed pumps.
  • Salt Systems: Enjoy soft, smooth water with our saltwater systems that provide a more natural swimming experience.
  • Filter Systems: Ensure your pool water stays clean and safe with high-performance filter systems.
  • In Ground LED Replacement Lights: Brighten up your pool and garden with energy-efficient LED lighting, perfect for nighttime enjoyment.
  • Heaters (Gas & Electric): Extend your swimming season with our high-quality pool heaters, available in both gas and electric options.
